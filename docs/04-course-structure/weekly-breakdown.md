# VII. Weekly Breakdown

This chapter provides a detailed week-by-week schedule designed to guide students through the comprehensive material of this book on Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics. The structured breakdown ensures a progressive learning experience, building foundational knowledge before advancing to more complex topics.

Each week's section will outline the specific learning objectives, recommended readings from the respective chapters, and suggested practical exercises or mini-projects. This systematic approach aims to help students manage their workload effectively, track their progress, and reinforce their understanding of both theoretical concepts and their practical application in robotics.
