This is the first chapter of our blog series on Humanoid Robotics. In this introduction, we will cover the basics of humanoid robots, their history, and their potential future applications.

Humanoid robots are robots built to resemble the human body. They are designed to interact with human tools and environments, making them ideal for tasks in human-centric spaces.

Stay tuned for more chapters where we will delve deeper into the fascinating world of humanoid robotics!
